About Us

Vermicompost Natural Manure is a sustainable agricultural enterprise specializing in the production of premium-grade vermicompost. Using specialized red wiggler earthworms, we convert organic waste into nutrient-dense organic fertilizer. Our mission is to enrich soil health, improve crop yields, and promote eco-friendly farming practices for commercial growers and home gardeners alike.
